## Minimum campaign controls

- Identify the sender and organisation accurately.
- Use only public, opt-in, existing-relationship or otherwise approved contact routes.
- Keep one suppression list across senders and vendors.
- Honour opt-outs immediately.
- Do not imply diagnosis, clinical efficacy, regulatory approval or superior outcomes without authorised evidence.
- Stop distribution when an accountable reviewer, claim source or privacy basis is missing.
- Track qualified organisation visits, completed evaluations, workshop registrations and pilot requests—not raw email volume.
