FAQ

Before you activate.

When does the US$10 charge begin?

Free Q-PGA access runs through 15 July 2026 at 23:59 Singapore time. The one-time US$10 charge starts 16 July 2026 at 00:00 Singapore time. It is not a subscription and does not renew automatically.

Does the agent send email automatically?

No. It drafts local materials only. Every external asset requires human, medical, legal, regulatory and privacy review appropriate to the audience and jurisdiction.

Can the public version verify payment securely?

No. GitHub Pages is static. This founding launch uses a soft browser marker after the hosted checkout returns. A production paid service must verify a signed payment webhook and authorise an authenticated account server-side.

How does the campaign reach pharmaceutical organisations?

Through public product discovery, industry communities, opt-in events, partners, existing relationships and relevant organisation-level outreach. It does not scrape or promise mass unsolicited messaging to every organisation.
