# QAGE² Pharma Growth — 60-Day Freemium Campaign Playbook

## Objective

Recruit a first cohort of pharmaceutical, biotech, CRO/CDMO, medical-affairs and translational-research organisations into a governed public sandbox, then qualify private institutional pilots.

The phrase **“all pharmaceutical organisations”** is implemented as broad public availability plus segmented, permission-led account development. It is not permission to scrape or indiscriminately email every organisation or employee.

## Offer

### Public Research Explorer — always free

- QAGE² research-module preview;
- governance and claim boundaries;
- research-only product walkthrough; and
- no payment or patient-data intake.

### Q-PGA Founding Agent Access — dated offer

- free through **15 July 2026 at 23:59 SGT**;
- **US$10 one-time payment** from **16 July 2026 at 00:00 SGT**;
- no subscription or automatic renewal;
- unlimited local briefs after founding activation;
- claim-risk scan and launch planning; and
- provider-hosted checkout.

### Enterprise Governance

Custom private deployment with server-verified entitlement, SSO/RBAC, audit history, evidence controls and medical/legal/regulatory workflow integration.

## Priority segments

1. Global and specialty pharmaceutical R&D / translational teams.
2. Medical-affairs and scientific-communications teams.
3. Digital-innovation and responsible-AI programmes.
4. Biotech business-development and platform teams.
5. CRO / CDMO innovation and partnership teams.
6. Academic translational centres and public research programmes.
7. Industry associations, accelerators and innovation networks.

## Positioning

**Category:** governed research-to-market workspace.

**Promise:** translate complex exploratory models into clear, evidence-aware, human-reviewable campaign and partnership materials.

**Boundary:** research workflow only; no patient care, no PHI, no autonomous sending, no unsupported clinical or regulatory claims.

## Launch sequence

### Days 1–7 — public launch

- merge the launch branch;
- enable GitHub Pages with GitHub Actions;
- configure the live US$10 one-time Payment Link;
- add recommended GitHub topics;
- publish a founder launch post and short walkthrough;
- invite existing collaborators to test the free agent before 15 July; and
- open the institutional-pilot issue form.

### Days 8–21 — education and discovery

- publish two educational posts per week on evidence classification, human-in-the-loop marketing and research-demo boundaries;
- share in suitable innovation, responsible-AI, biotech and open-source communities;
- invite associations and accelerators through public partnership channels; and
- open registration for an opt-in evidence workshop.

### Days 22–35 — opt-in event

Workshop: **From exploratory biomedical models to reviewable pharmaceutical narratives**.

Agenda:

1. research output versus promotional claim;
2. evidence taxonomy;
3. QAGE² research preview;
4. Q-PGA campaign build;
5. claim gate and human approval; and
6. institutional deployment requirements.

### Days 36–60 — account-based pilots

- prioritise organisations that visited, registered, referred or replied;
- use existing relationships, partners and public business channels;
- send only small, reviewed sequences where an appropriate contact basis exists;
- suppress opt-outs across all senders and vendors;
- invite qualified teams into a scoped institutional evaluation; and
- document security, integration and governance requirements.

## Founder launch copy

> Complex biomedical innovation deserves better than either hype or incomprehensible detail. We are opening QAGE² Pharma Growth: a public research preview plus a human-reviewed agent for evidence-aware positioning, campaign planning and institutional pilots. It does not diagnose, accept patient-identifiable data or send outreach autonomously. The research explorer remains free. Q-PGA is free through 15 July 2026; from 16 July it is available for a one-time US$10 payment with no subscription.

## Campaign KPIs

- qualified organisation visits;
- free early-access activations;
- checkout views and completed one-time payments;
- free-to-paid conversion after 16 July 2026;
- campaign briefs completed;
- workshop registrations and attendance;
- institutional pilot requests;
- qualified replies and meetings;
- opt-out and complaint rate;
- risky claims blocked before publication; and
- pilot-to-private-deployment conversion.

## Stop conditions

Pause a campaign when a material claim lacks a source or owner, an asset implies clinical use or efficacy, patient data enter the workflow, opt-outs are not honoured, complaint rates rise, a vendor cannot apply the suppression list, or an accountable human reviewer cannot be identified.
